COVID-19

Due to COVID-19, all airlines have waived cancellation and changing fees, so you can have peace of mind.

Certain countries require that tourists present a negative PCR COVID test taken no more than 48 hours before landing. Please check with the country you are traveling to in order to make sure you have all the paperwork you need.

If you catch COVID while visiting a country, we ask that you follow all safety protocols that have been put in place in the country you're visiting. Your health and safety are very important to us.

We require all of our customers to wear a mask while at the airport. We understand that not everyone will agree with this rule and will gladly issue a full refund to anyone who asks for one.

General Questions

We pride ourselves on having excellent customer service. If you have missed your flight, simply contact us and we will find a solution for you.

While we don't charge for checked luggage, the airline companies may, and the customer is responsible for that fee.

We're sorry you won't be joining us on this trip. If there is anything we can do to change your mind, let us know and we will do everything we can do to ensure your satisfaction. However, if there isn't anything we can do, we will gladly find a solution. Please note that certain fees may apply.
